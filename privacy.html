Privacy Policy

Introduction

We at Wanderlust Travels value your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you book a tour or fill out a contact form on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process bookings, send confirmations, and communicate important updates about your trip. We may also send promotional offers, but you can opt out of these communications at any time.

3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless it is necessary for the completion of your booking or required by law.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience on our website. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to do so, please contact us through the contact page on our website.
