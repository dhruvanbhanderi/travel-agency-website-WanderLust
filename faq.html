Frequently Asked Questions

What are the payment options?

We accept payments via credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and UPI. You can also pay through online payment gateways such as PayPal.

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour directly on our website by selecting a tour package and filling out the booking form. Alternatively, you can contact us through email or phone to book your tour.

Is travel insurance included in the packages?

Travel insurance is not included in the standard tour packages. However, we recommend purchasing travel insurance separately for coverage during your trip.

What should I bring on my trip?

We recommend bringing comfortable clothing, a valid passport (for international trips), and any necessary medications. We also suggest carrying a camera to capture the memories!

Can I change or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can change or cancel your booking, but please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on the tour package and the time of cancellation. Please refer to our terms and conditions for more details.
